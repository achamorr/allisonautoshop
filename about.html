Allison Auto Center is a family owned and operated full-service automotive repair shop located in Ossining, New York. We offer a wide variety of auto repair and maintenance. Our goal is to provide the highest quality of work coupled with the best customer service when it comes to your vehicle’s needs. Your vehicle is a huge investment, and we understand this. This is why we want to make sure that you get the most out of your investment by enhancing your vehicle’s life, reducing risks and potential future costs, and providing quality after-market accessories to personalize your vehicle. At AAC, we know the importance of treating people with honesty, integrity, and care. We strive to provide excellent customer service and the highest quality of work possible. We are always happy to answer your questions and guide you in the right direction.
